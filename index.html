    Trait Symbiosis

    There will be special type bunnies that you can change the trait with the help of other bunnies. You can access this feature through our backstage.

    Backstage

    Here you can try various things, such as seeing the bunnies you have, peek rarity, trying out various costumes, do trait symbiotic process, and so on.

    Access Ticket

    By having a bunny, you will get access to our events and beneficial rewards. You will also be at the front row of our upcoming projects.

    Unique Design

    This project is designed in a unique 3D artstyle and created based on hundreds of traits. Thus, each token will be different from the others.

    Investment

    Hold your bunnies and watch the market grow. This project is designed for the long term.

    Community Based

    This project is community based. Determine the future direction of this project together. Build a solid community in the Metaverse.
