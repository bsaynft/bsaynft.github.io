    • What's this?

      This is an NFT project created with the aim of building a solid community and sharing positive vibes through the NFT itself.

      NFT stands for non-fungible token. NFT is a financial security consisting of digital data stored in a blockchain, a form of distributed ledger. The ownership of an NFT is recorded in the blockchain, and can be transferred by the owner, allowing NFTs to be sold and traded.

    • What can I do with this NFT?

      As the authentic owner of your NFT, you are free to do anything with your NFT. You can sell, save or give it to your friends.

      You can keep this NFT as an investment and use it to access our various utilities and events in the future. You can also use it as a profile picture on your social media accounts.

    • Where can I get my Bunny?

      You can get your Bunny from our public sale session by minting your NFT using your Metamask wallet. It will cost you 10 Matic per one NFT and a very small amount of gas fee. Another way is to visit our marketplace on OpenSea and buy a minted NFT.

    • What does public sale mean? what is mint?

      Public sale is a session where we sell our NFT to the public. In this session, you can get your NFT at the floor price by minting.

      Minting is the process of printing your token into the blockchain. So that can be displayed as an NFT. It will cost you 10 Matic per one NFT you mint and a very small amount of gas fee.

    • Where can I see the Bunny I've bought?

      You can see it on your OpenSea profile as well as on our marketplace. As we will open our collection in other marketplaces, you will also be able to see your Bunny there.

    • On what blockchain is this project running on?

      Our collection is on the Polygon blockchain. The reason we use the Polygon blockchain is because this chain requires very low gas fees, lots of features and also the thriving community. Suit best for our project.

    • I am required to connect my wallet to this site, is it safe?

      Connection your wallet to this site is required when you make a transaction or when this site is requires data from your wallet to be read. We do not have the ability to take control of your wallet. We are able to submit transaction requests to you wallet, but all control is completely yours.

      We are also 100% transparent. All data from this site is hosted on our Github Repository. Anyone can view the source code that run this site in real-time.

    • Where can I get service assistance regarding to this project?

      You can go to our Discord server and submit your support ticket. Feel free to join, it's for everyone :D
